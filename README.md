## The Origins of Peruvian Cocaine

In the 20th century, the production of cocaine hydrochloride escalated due to global demand, making Peru a significant source in South America.

By 2026, international efforts like those from the UN and local governments have intensified anti-trafficking measures. Understanding this history helps contextualize the dangers without endorsing any illicit trade.

### Key Historical Milestones

- **1970s-1980s Boom**: Rise of cartels and international smuggling routes.
- **Modern Crackdowns**: Enhanced border controls and crop substitution programs in Peru.
